# Builders Can Ship Faster Than They Can Get Customers

Status: Draft
Platform: X
Scheduled: 2026-06-09 16:10 Europe/Berlin
Pillar: Building vs Distribution

## Draft

One thing that feels increasingly true:

Builders can now create products faster than they can get customers.

Coding agents compress the time from idea to working software.

But the market does not compress at the same rate.

You still have to find people, understand them, earn attention, and learn what they actually care about.

Feels like the bottleneck is moving.
